## 🛤 Critical Path
1. **Cognitive Load** *(root_cause)*

2. **Cognitive Process** *(mechanism)*
   When cognitive load is reduced, the mental loop of processing thoughts, stress, and ideas can function properly — this is the engine that powers both emotional well-being and strategic awareness.
3. **Scenario Awareness** *(moderator)*
   Functioning cognition produces a clear-eyed view of realistic outcome ranges — worst case, optimal, and ideal — which is the foundation of good decision-making and expectation alignment.
4. **Decision Quality** *(moderator)*
   Knowing the full landscape of possible outcomes makes any major career or life choice more risk-aware and better matched to personal goals.
5. **Job Change or Startup Decision** *(mechanism)*
   The pivotal career decision — shaped by work dissatisfaction, scenario clarity, and available assets — is the action that opens or closes the path to work fulfillment and passive income.
6. **Work Satisfaction** *(mechanism)*
   Fulfillment from work is both a direct amplifier of happiness and a trigger for change when it is absent — making it simultaneously a driver of well-being and a catalyst for the career decision.
7. **Happiness** *(effect)*
   Happiness is the convergence point of supportive thinking, satisfying work, good health, and expectations that match lived reality — all reinforcing each other over time.
